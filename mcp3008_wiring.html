⚠️ Tips:

• Place MCP3008 chip across the center gap of the breadboard (pins on both sides)
• Use the notch/dot on the MCP3008 to find Pin 1 (top-left)
• Connect AO pin (not DO) from the Grove Sound Sensor
• Double-check 3.3V — do NOT use 5V for VREF or it may damage readings
